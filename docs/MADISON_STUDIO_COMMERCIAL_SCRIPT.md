# Madison Studio Commercial Script

## Concept: Teach Madison Once. Create Everywhere.

**Length:** 60 seconds  
**Format:** Works as 16:9 website hero video, 9:16 paid social, and 1:1 retargeting cutdown  
**Audience:** Beauty, fragrance, personal care, and luxury ecommerce founders or marketing teams who need consistent content without building a full in-house agency  
**Tone:** Calm authority, refined warmth, strategic precision  
**Core promise:** Madison becomes the brand-trained editorial director that turns scattered ideas into consistent, on-brand content across every channel.

### Creative Direction

The spot should feel like a premium editorial workspace coming alive. Start with the friction of modern content work: too many channels, too many drafts, too little consistency. Then show Madison absorbing the brand, creating master content, multiplying it into native formats, and organizing the whole system into a usable content operation.

Use real app footage wherever possible. The strongest visual path is:

1. Dashboard and Brand Health Score
2. Brand DNA scan or Brand Guidelines upload
3. Forge/Create generation
4. Madison editorial refinement or agent progress indicators
5. Multiply derivatives
6. Image Studio/Dark Room product visuals
7. Library and Calendar scheduling
8. Final Madison logo/CTA

Recommended demo brand: **Luna & Sage**, matching the existing tutorial strategy.

## Shot List

### Essential App Footage

- Dashboard with Brand Health, Content Pipeline, and recent activity visible
- Brand Guidelines upload or Brand DNA website scan
- Brand Quick View panel with colors, fonts, logo, and tone/squad details
- Forge/Create form being completed
- Generated content appearing in the output panel
- Madison editorial assistant refining a headline or paragraph
- Multiply transforming master content into multiple derivative folders
- Video script derivative or Video Project screen for the "video" moment
- Image Studio/Dark Room product visual generation
- Library content grid showing saved assets
- Calendar with scheduled content

### Transitional B-Roll

- Cursor moving from notes to Madison dashboard
- Product detail shots for the demo brand
- Editorial desk details: paper, pen, product, laptop, warm light
- Quick cuts of email, Instagram, Shopify/Etsy, and TikTok surfaces
- Final calm dashboard state with no visual clutter

---

## Visual Style

Use Madison's existing brand system:

- **Palette:** Vellum cream, parchment white, ink black, aged brass
- **Typography mood:** Editorial serif for major claims, restrained sans-serif for UI captions
- **Motion:** Slow pans, soft zooms, crisp UI highlights, restrained transitions
- **Avoid:** loud neon SaaS graphics, hustle language, exaggerated AI magic, generic stock footage

The commercial should feel premium and capable, not flashy. Madison is the calm operator in the room.

---

## Production Notes

- Use a polished fictitious brand such as **Luna & Sage** so the commercial can show strong brand DNA without exposing real client data.
- Capture real UI footage at 1080p or higher. For social cutdowns, record enough negative space for 9:16 crops.
- Keep the Brand Health and Multiply moments visually clear. Those are the two easiest "aha" moments for new viewers.
- Show one clean before/after: generic content before Madison, brand-aligned content after Madison.
- Keep on-screen text sparse. Let the UI prove the product.
- End on a clear CTA: **Start creating with Madison Studio** or **Create your first piece**.
